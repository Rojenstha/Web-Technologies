Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an open world MMORPG game which falls in the category of dark souls game full of adventures, exploration and action. Here we can customzied our own character and head out into the vast world of Elden Ring to become the next elden lord as a tarnished. We get to choose amongst 10 classes as a started ie, Vagabond, Prisoner, Astrologer, Hero, Bandit, Warrior, Confessor , Prophet, Samurai and Wretch each with a different starting stats. We explore the vast world as we progress through the story leveling up and fighting against bosses who are super tough. With many quests, side quests and an interesting storyline with 5 different endings, winning "The best game award of the year 2022" is really fun to play for people who love MMORPG openworld daarksoul adventure action games.
